Podcasts

An eight-episode series where blind storytellers put the tools into practice.

Blind people are often discussed. Here, they are heard.

How it works

  1. 01

    Open call

    We invite blind creators who want to make audio, whether they already have a polished idea or just a sound they cannot stop thinking about.

  2. 02

    Hardware and mentoring

    Participants receive Blind Envy hardware and three months of hands-on mentoring in the device and in sound production, with teachers including Elyse Blennerhassett, trained in oral history and Columbia's Journalism and Sound Art programs, and Ariana Martinez, a Rutgers Media Arts lecturer and Disabled Journalists Association fellow.

  3. 03

    Release

    We help finish the work and support distribution wherever it can travel: podcast platforms, festivals, and other listening spaces.
